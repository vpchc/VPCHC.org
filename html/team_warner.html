Jan Warner, MAC

Education

  • University of Missouri – St. Louis
  • St. Louis Christian College
  • Lincoln Christian University

Certifications

  • National Emergency Medical Technician

Experience

As a mental health provider at Valley Professionals Community Health Center, Jan serves and assists patients with behavioral treatment. Her past experience includes the United States Air Force, Richland Community College, Herington Counseling and Learning Alternatives, and Pleasant Meadows Christian Village.

Jan’s military experience and being a veteran of the Gulf War era, brings a unique understanding of the military lifestyle and the difficulties military soldiers, veterans and their family’s experience, not only on a daily basis, but also during war time. Jan is passionate about helping those who have served our country and their families.
 

Professional Organizations

  • American Association of Christian Counseling
  • American Counseling Association


Personal/interests

Jan enjoys spending time with her children and grandchildren, enjoying a good movie, making homemade cards, and scrapbooking family pictures. She also enjoys attending her son’s and grandson’s sporting events and school functions.
