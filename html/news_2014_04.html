Valley Professionals Community Health Center Leading in Innovation

March 4, 2014

Valley Professionals Community Health Center was recently featured in the 1st quarter edition of the 2014 Indiana State Department of Health’s Chronic Disease Newsletter. The article highlights VPCHC and the ways it continues to improve the health and care of those in the community. The VPCHC article can be viewed on page 4, through the link below.
 
The Division of Chronic Disease Prevention and Control, Division of Nutrition and Physical Activity, Office of Primary Care and State Office of Rural Health and the Tobacco Prevention Cessation Commission are pleased to release the 1st quarter edition of the 2014 Indiana State Department of Health’s Chronic Disease Newsletter. This publication is part of our ongoing efforts to bring together all partners and stakeholders interested in reducing the burden of chronic disease through prevention, early screening, and best practices in treatment.

View the full 1st Quarter Chronic Disease Newsletter PDF online (see article on page 4).
