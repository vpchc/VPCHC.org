Dr. Julia Wernz, Ph.D., HSPP

Education

  • PhD — Indiana State University
  • MS — Indiana State University
  • BA — Oral Roberts University

Certifications

  • HSPP (Health Service Provider in Psychology)
  • EMDR Certified

Experience

Dr. Wernz has been a practicing Psychologist since 2007. Her past experience includes working with severely emotionally-disordered children and their families in an outpatient setting, as well as inpatient and health psychology settings. Dr. Wernz has practiced more as a generalist in the field for the past four years, treating patients with attention deficit, pervasive developmental, mood, anxiety and personality disorders, while collaborating with medical providers and other service providers to comprehensively treat patients. Dr. Wernz created the behavioral health integration model for the VPCHC and services, as the Director of Behavioral Health for all locations.

Personal/interests

Dr. Wernz enjoys spending time with family and friends, being outdoors, camping, hiking and reading.
