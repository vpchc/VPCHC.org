New Doctor Joins Local Health Center

January 5, 2012

The Vermillion-Parke Community Health Center would like to welcome Family Practitioner, Elaine Carlson, M.D.

Dr. Carlson is a 2008 graduate of the University of Massachusetts Medical School, and completed her undergraduate studies at Sarah Lawrence College, in Bronxville, New York.

Dr. Carlson completed her medical residency training through Union Hospital Family Medicine Residency Program in Terre Haute, in 2011. She has also completed medical rotations at the Vermillion-Parke Community Health Center during her residency.

Dr. Carlson enjoys practicing in family medicine, because “When you have the opportunity to provide care to an entire family, you are truly able to know the patient as a whole.” Other clinical interests include women’s health, maternal child health, adolescent health, and international medicine.

“Since Dr. Carlson completed her residency rotations at our clinic, we knew she would be a great fit for our clinic and our community. She is committed to her patients and their healthcare. We are honored to have such a wonderful physician to add to our staff,” stated Elizabeth Burrows, CEO of VPCHC.

Prior to medical school, Dr. Carlson served in the Peace Corps as a Community Health Agent in a rural village in Niger, West Africa. She also worked in Public Health in the city of Boston, with the Haitian community on HIV prevention initiatives.

Dr. Carlson enjoys long-distance backpacking, hiking, and the outdoors. She has hiked the 2,000-plus mile Appalachian Trail from Maine to Georgia three times, most recently in 2011. She has also recently run the Tecumseh Trail Marathon.

Dr. Carlson will be seeing patients at the VPCHC Clinton office, and providing services during the VPCHC After-Hours Clinic. The health center is open Monday through Friday from 8:00 a.m. to 5:00 p.m. The After-Hours Clinic is open Monday through Thursday from 5:00 p.m. to 8:00 p.m.at the Clinton facility. If you have any questions, or would like to schedule an appointment, call the Clinton office at (765) 832-1003.
