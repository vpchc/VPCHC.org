“The Vermillion-Parke Community Health Center has been a wonderful addition to the community. When I found VPCHC and began to utilize the facility and its providers, I was impressed by the quality of care and the helpfulness of the staff. For such a small community, we are lucky to have such a place.

I could not be more proud to recommend VPCHC to everyone I know.”

- Patient of VPCHC
