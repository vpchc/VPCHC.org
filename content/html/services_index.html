Mobile School-Based Health Center
The Mobile School-Based Health Center (MSBHC) is a mobile medical clinic that will travel to all 14 schools in the five school corporations of Parke and Vermillion counties. The mobile clinic will provide a full range of health care services so students can avoid health-related absences and get additional support to succeed in the classroom. The MSBHC will provide scheduled, on-site access during the academic day when school is in session. Read more about the services and benefits the MSBHC has to offer, by clicking on the link below!

Telemedicine Health Services
For the convenience our patients, Vermillion-Parke Community Health Center offers Telemedicine health services at its Clinton and Cayuga, Indiana locations. Telemedicine is the use of video conferencing to enable healthcare providers at a different location, to provide health care treatment to you and/or consult with you and your healthcare provider about your health care options and decisions. Behavioral health and cardiology telemedicine services are currently available for health center patients.
